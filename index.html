About

I am a doctoral researcher in Measurement, Evaluation, Statistics & Assessment with an MBA and an M.S. in Computer Science, and seven-plus years building reporting, assessment, and analytics systems across higher education and healthcare.

My work combines rigorous quantitative methods and program evaluation with hands-on data engineering to turn complex data into decisions institutions can act on. I study how technology shapes learning, STEM education, and workforce development — and its effects on educational outcomes and the broader economy.
